    <h2>Introduction</h2>
    <p>Voice-first AI represents a paradigm shift in how we interact with artificial intelligence. Instead of typing commands or navigating complex interfaces, users can simply speak naturally to their AI assistant. This is particularly significant in India's diverse linguistic landscape where Hinglish (Hindi + English) and various regional languages are commonly used in daily communication.</p>
    <p>PersonalAI.cloud's voice-first capabilities enable seamless interaction across multiple Indian languages, breaking down language barriers and making AI accessible to a broader population.</p>
  </section>

  <h2>Why Voice-First Matters for India</h2>
  <p>India has 22 official languages and hundreds of dialects. Most people are more comfortable speaking in their native language than typing in English. Voice-first AI bridges this gap by allowing natural conversation in any language.</p>

  <h2>Key Features</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Multi-language Support:</strong> Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, and many more Indian languages.</li>
    <li><strong>Hinglish Understanding:</strong> Natural code-switching between Hindi and English.</li>
    <li><strong>On-Device Processing:</strong> Privacy-preserving speech recognition.</li>
    <li><strong>Offline Capability:</strong> Works without internet connectivity.</li>
  </ul>
